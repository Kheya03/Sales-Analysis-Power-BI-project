#  Sales Overview Dashboard | Excel, Power BI

An interactive **Power BI Sales Dashboard** developed to analyze sales performance across different regions, states, and years. The dashboard enables business users to compare current and previous year performance, identify geographical sales trends, monitor profitability, and support data-driven decision making through interactive visualizations.

---

##  Project Overview

Businesses generate thousands of sales transactions every day. However, without proper reporting and visualization, it becomes difficult to identify business trends, monitor regional performance, and make strategic decisions.

This project demonstrates how **Power BI** can transform raw sales data into meaningful business insights by building an interactive dashboard that compares **Sales, Profit, and Quantity** across multiple years and geographical regions.

The dashboard provides stakeholders with an easy-to-use reporting solution to evaluate business performance and identify growth opportunities.

---

#  Business Case Study

## Background

A nationwide retail company operates across four major regions:

- Central
- East
- South
- West

Although the company has experienced continuous growth, management still relies on static Excel reports to monitor business performance.

This reporting approach makes it difficult to:

- Track yearly business growth
- Compare regional performance
- Monitor profitability
- Identify declining markets
- Understand geographical sales distribution
- Make quick strategic decisions

As a result, decision-making becomes slow and reactive instead of proactive.

---

## Business Problem

The leadership team wants a centralized reporting solution capable of answering questions such as:

- Which region generates the highest revenue?
- Which states contribute the most sales?
- Is the business growing compared to previous years?
- Which regions require immediate attention?
- How does product demand vary across locations?
- Which months perform the best?
- Where should future marketing investments be focused?

Without these insights, allocating resources efficiently and planning future business strategies becomes challenging.

---

## Proposed Solution

To solve these business challenges, an interactive **Power BI Sales Overview Dashboard** was developed.

The dashboard enables users to:

- Compare Current Year (CY) and Previous Year (PY) performance
- Analyze Year-over-Year (YoY) growth
- Monitor regional sales performance
- Visualize state-wise sales using maps
- Compare profitability across regions
- Analyze monthly sales trends
- Dynamically switch between Sales, Profit, and Quantity KPIs

The dashboard transforms transactional sales data into meaningful business intelligence that supports strategic decision-making.
